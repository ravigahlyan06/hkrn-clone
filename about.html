About Us

Haryana Kaushal Rozgar Nigam Limited has been incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 on 13th October, 2021. It has been set up with the objective of providing contractual manpower to all Government entities in Haryana in a transparent, robust and equitable manner. It will act as the authorized agency for providing contractual manpower in Haryana

Haryana Kaushal Rozgar Nigam, in the process of providing contractual manpower, will focus on :

  • Uplifting socio-economically disadvantaged candidates
  • Facilitating timely payment of salary and benefits to deployed manpower
  • Ensuring adherence to the State Reservation policy
